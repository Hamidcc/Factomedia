2024 Summer Olympics

The 2024 Summer Olympics will be held in Paris, France, from July 26 to August 11, marking the third time the city has hosted the games. This event will feature 329 events across 32 sports and is expected to involve 10,714 athletes from 206 nations​. This edition is significant not only because it returns to Paris after a 100-year gap (since 1924) but also because of the historic inclusion of new sports such as breaking, which debuted in 2024​.As for some specific details, the opening ceremony will take place on the Seine River, with a grand parade involving athletes along Paris’s iconic waterways​. The closing ceremony will be at the Stade de France​.
